Hi, it's Álvaro!

Robotics and tech enthusiast engineer with finance passion

I'm Álvaro Arnaiz, a 23-year-old telecommunications engineer from Madrid, Spain. I'm currently established in Lausanne, Switzerland, finishing my master's degree as an exchange student at EPFL. My whole life has been oriented to technological ambition to create and innovate, which has led to who I am.

My technical background provides me with a broad understanding of the whole tech market, as I possess a wide range of skills and knowledge that allow me adapting to different roles and challenges. Also, I love business, finance and investing in my free time, since I consider it a fundamental skill for my future.

I'm looking forward to learn about other people's experience and to make my own way in the industry.
